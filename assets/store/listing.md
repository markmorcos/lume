## 2. Full description — English (≤ 4000 chars)

```
You took fifteen near-identical selfies. Which one do you actually post?

LUMÉ is the calm, fast way to pick your best shot. Add up to 20 photos and LUMÉ
reads each one like a photo editor would — clarity, light and colour, facial
proportion, eye contact, smile and more — then hands you a ranked top three with
scores. No more second-guessing, no more group-chat polls.

100% ON YOUR DEVICE
Your photos never leave your phone. LUMÉ scores everything locally using
classic computer vision — no cloud, no uploads, no account. That means it's
private by design, works offline, and ranks 20 photos in seconds.

PICK FOR THE MOMENT
One tap tunes the judging to what the shot is for:
• Dating — smile, eye contact, warmth
• LinkedIn — clarity, proportion, composure
• Social — light & colour, good-hair-day
Fine-tune further with simple toggles to switch any criterion on or off, and
watch the ranking update instantly.

SEE WHY IT WON
Every result shows a clear overall score plus the per-criterion breakdown on
your winner — so you understand the pick, not just trust it. Save your favourite
straight to your library in a tap.

LUMÉ PREMIUM
Going further when it matters:
• Group photos — score the whole scene, who looks best together
• Posture & body language
• Custom weighting & watermark-free export
• History of every shortlist
$6.99/month with a 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime.

WHY ON-DEVICE (and why it matters)
• Private — personal photos stay on your phone
• Instant — local scoring powers the live results
• Free of per-use fees and works with no signal
• Consistent — the same photos always rank the same way

Stop agonising over the camera roll. Add your shots, pick the moment, and let
LUMÉ find the one.

Questions or feedback: <TBD: support email>
```

Character count: ~1,500 / 4,000 — room to add testimonials or seasonal copy.

## 9. What's left for you
1. Provide a **support email** and **developer/publisher name** (Play requires both).
2. Confirm `https://markmorcos.github.io/lume/privacy.html` is the policy URL you want (it deploys with the dashboard).
3. In Play Console: create the app, upload the AAB to Internal testing, paste §1–§2 copy, upload the icon/feature graphic/screenshots, complete Data safety (§7) + content rating, create the `lume.premium.monthly` subscription with the 7-day trial, then roll out.
