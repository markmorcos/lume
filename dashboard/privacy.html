LUMÉ — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

LUMÉ ("the app") helps you pick your best photo. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data. The short version: your photos never leave your device, and we do not collect personal data.

Your photos

When you select photos, LUMÉ analyses them entirely on your device using on-device computer vision. Your photos and the scores derived from them are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server. They stay in your phone's photo library and the app's local memory for the duration of the analysis.

Photo library permission

The app requests access to your photo library only so you can choose photos to analyse and save your chosen winner back to your library. You can grant full or limited access, and revoke it at any time in your device settings.

Data we collect

None that identifies you. LUMÉ has no account system and no server backend. The app does not collect names, emails, contacts, location, or device identifiers, and contains no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Subscriptions

LUMÉ Premium is an auto-renewing subscription processed by Google Play Billing. Google handles the transaction and your payment details under Google's Privacy Policy. LUMÉ only receives a signal from Google indicating whether your subscription is active; it never sees your payment information.

Children

LUMÉ is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new version will be posted at this URL.
